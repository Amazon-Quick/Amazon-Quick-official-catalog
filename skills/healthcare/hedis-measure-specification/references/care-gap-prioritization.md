# Care Gap Prioritization

## Prioritization decision tree

```
Is the measure triple-weighted for Star Ratings?
  YES -> High priority baseline
    Member high-risk (Charlson 3 or higher, or LACE 10 or higher)?
      YES -> Critical priority, immediate outreach
      NO  -> High priority, standard outreach
    Social determinants of health barriers (Z-codes, high Area Deprivation Index)?
      YES -> Assign care coordinator
      NO  -> Automated reminder is sufficient
  NO -> Standard priority
    More than 6 months remaining in the measurement year?
      YES -> Schedule in next outreach batch
      NO  -> Escalate if feasible
    Process measure (screening or test)?
      YES -> High closure probability, include
      NO  -> Outcome measure, coordinate with primary care provider
```

## Rate interpretation

| Rate range | Star level | Action |
|------------|-----------|--------|
| 90th percentile or above | 5-star | Maintain current programs |
| 75th to 89th | 4-star | Targeted improvement |
| 50th to 74th | 3-star | Systematic outreach needed |
| 25th to 49th | 2-star | Intensive intervention, root cause analysis |
| Below 25th | 1-star | Urgent remediation, leadership escalation |

## Watch out

- Do not treat all measures with equal priority. Triple-weighted Star Rating
  measures carry three times the impact and come first.
